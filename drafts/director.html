Director

By: Mark Drummond On: Sat 01 June 2019
In: Posts
Tags: #identity #iam

I feel like I have arrived someplace important. I am very excited to report that I have been made the Director of Identity and Access Management at my place of work. Identity, mainly 'customer' identity, has been part of job for many years. I've been working with the old Sun Directory, Identity, and Access Management solutions going back to when Sun's Directory Server was the Netscape Directory Server. At the same time, my involvement and exposure to the identity space has been quite constrained: LDAP directories, Sun's Access Manager, and Sun's Identity Manager.
