Old Pen Day - Pilot Metropolitan MR

By: Mark Drummond On: Thu 04 November 2021
In: Pen Life
Tags: #fountain-pens #npd

The OG GOAT. The first proper fountain pen I bought in relatively recent times.

I usually only feature my higher end (whatever that means) pens on here, but the Metro definitely deserves some love. I might think of my LAMY 2000 as "the GOAT" but it is really hard to beat the Pilot Metropolitan. At about $30 CAD, with a fantastic Pilot 'F' nib on it, you could buy and use no other pen and be perfectly happy.

I'm not a huge fan of the tapered cigar style, but the Metro feels great in the hand. It has some weight to it, unlike the Platinum Plaisir which, despite also being made of metal, is as light as a feather. I prefer the Metro in this regard.
