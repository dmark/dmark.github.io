Cloud and Identity Systems Lead

Posted on June 20, 2018 in All The Posts

A new shift in my responsibilities at work, with more focus on two key aspects of our environment that I have been leading on the operations side of things: Consolidating our cloud computing resources on Google Cloud Platform and our migration to our new Identity as a Service partner, Auth0.

This is a welcome change. The nature of my work is such that I tend to be pulled in many different directions at once (this is what happens when you are interested in and develop expertise in everything under the Sun). That is still the case now, but at least recognizing these two keys areas does help to stay on target.
