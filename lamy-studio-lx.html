New Pen Day - LAMY Studio LX All Black

By: Mark Drummond On: Thu 08 July 2021
In: Pen Life
Tags: #fountain-pens #npd

I really enjoy my LAMY Studio Imperial Blue pen. I like it so much I even replaced the steel nib with a gold nib. But the chromed section makes it a bit of a challenge to use for extended periods of writing. The LX All Black edition has a rubberized section that provides plenty of grip. And it looks amazing. In fact I plan to replace its steel nib with a gold as well.

Purchased from: Wonderpens
