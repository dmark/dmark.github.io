The Books I Read in 2018

Posted on December 31, 2018 in An Avid Reader

Early in the year I set out to read 24 books (two per month) in 2018. I actually blew past 24 books fairly quickly, in part due to Andrzej Sapkowski’s “The Witcher” series books1 which are pretty quick reads, so I changed my goal to 36 books (three per month).

Down to the wire, I squeezed out the last book today, December 31st. Next year I’ll go back to 24 books and if I beat that goal, great, but 36 is a bit much with everything else I have on my plate. I readily admit I started binging the Harry Potter books, quick reads like the Witcher books, over the Christmas holiday season to help get me to 36.
