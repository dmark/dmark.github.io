The Viking Tour - Day 10

By: Mark Drummond On: Sun 12 August 2018
In: Motorrad
Tags: #motorsports

Channel-Port aux Basques to Antigonish. We altered our return ferry to cross in the daytime. We also got lucky and we able to upgrade to a berth so we could at least try to nap. Still not the best way to do the crossing on motorcycles since you are still tired and it is late in the day when you land so we were racing to our next motel, Homeward Inns of Canada, another great motorcycle friendly location.
