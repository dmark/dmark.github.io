AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate

Posted on Fri 26 August 2016 in All The Posts

Another certification in the bag. I wrote the AWS Associate level Solutions Architect exam today. I didn’t realize AWS certs are only good for 2 years. Sets a bit of a roadmap for my AWS certifications going forward. I got my Associate level SysOps cert in earl 2015, so I’ll want to replace it with the Professional level DevOps cert next year, and I’ll want to replace my Associate level Architect cert with the Professional level cert in 2018.
