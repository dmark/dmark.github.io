New Pen Day - Sailor Pro Gear

Thu 18 November 2021

I recently passed my CISSP exam and rewarded myself with this Goulet Pens exclusive Stealth Green Sailor Pro Gear with a medium-fine nib.

This pen is gorgeous. It is however quite short. It's not much longer than a Pilot Prera which is a pocket pen. It actually fits my hand just fine. I just need to get used to it. It's more like my brain knows the pen is shorter than my other full-size pens and is calling it out.

Goulet always includes a Tootsie Pop and "that's what I appreciates about them."

I really don't want to be one of those people that upgrades to every new generation of iPhone, but macro mode on the iPhone 13 sure would be handy here.

The nib was also slightly off. Under a loupe it looks like the left tine was just a hair lower than the right, and I was getting what is almost certainly excessive scratch going left to right. Doing the "flex-and-roll" trick seems to be working so there should be no need for a nibsmith. Mind you this is a 21k gold nib and I am rather nervous about adjusting it myself.

Purchased from: Goulet
