About

mark [AT] markdrummond [DOT] ca

I am a career IT systems analyst, specializing in cloud and identity systems, mainly Google Cloud Platform, Amazon Web Services, and Identity as a Service platform Auth0. Other services I work with on a regular and frequent basis include Apigee, Cloudflare, Entrust Datacard, EasyDNS, Acquia, Pantheon, Heroku, and Sendgrid. My IT systems expertise, spanning over 22 years, is founded on Linux and Unix systems administration, TCP/IP networking and services, system & network security, identity & access management, and LDAP directory services.

I am also a part-time undergraduate student at Queen’s University, working on an Honours B.A. in Applied Economics.

My professional interests include:

  • Cloud technologies including Google Cloud Platform, Kubernetes, Docker
  • Identity and access management technologies including Auth0 and OAuth 2.0
  • Pretending to be a Python programmer

I keep a reasonably up-to-date resume on this site. More details are available from my various online presences linked in the sidebar.

I’m a big fan of riding motorcycles.
