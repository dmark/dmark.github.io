New Pen Day - Pilot Custom 74

Fri 29 October 2021

New Pen Day is always a good day! I was never really much interested in the Pilot Custom 74. The 92 and the 823 were of greater interest mainly because of their full-body fill mechanisms.

That was until Pilot came out with this seasonally inspired Blue Stone version. The 74 comes in many colours, none of which were terribly exciting to me. But this Blue Stone model definitely caught my eye. And I am happy to say it is as good looking in person as it was in the photos.
