Ghost of Yotei

By: Mark Drummond On: Sun 02 November 2025
In: Vidya
Tags: #vidya

The Highs

  • The story is not as compelling as that of Ghost of Tsushima, but it is still excellent.
  • The combat: Atsu's multiple weapons makes for combat that is even more satisfying than Ghost of Tsushima, and that is saying a great deal.

The Sighs

  • Again, the fetch quests.

Worth It?

100%.
