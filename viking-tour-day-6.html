The Viking Tour - Day 6

By: Mark Drummond On: Wed 08 August 2018
In: Motorrad
Tags: #motorsports

With the Harley in the shop getting fixed up we took one of those four wheeled vehicular contraptions to do some touring. We took the 340 North to the Beothuk Interpretation Centre (the Beothuk were the indigenous population in Newfoundland). The picture below is the waterfront at the Interpretaion Centre.

From there we went to the Long Point Lighthouse. The Lighthouse was closed but the scenery is pretty amazing.

Repping Auth0 on The Rock.

On our way back we stopped for some lobster. Lobster season was over but we were able to find som decent sized critters for dinner.
