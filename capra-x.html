Anlas Capra X

By: Mark Drummond On: Mon 22 July 2019
In: Live to Ride, Ride to Live
Tags: #motorsports #reviews

With plans to do some off-road stuff on my moto, I bought some appropriate rubber. I posted a bit of a running review over on the R1200GS forums. The Coles Notes version is: I don't have much off-road experience to compare these tires to, but they seemed to work quite well for my puposes. They handled deep sand and mud with ease, and they seem to be well behaved and pretty comfortable on paved roads as well.
