My 2016 BMW R1200GS

Posted on Tue 13 September 2016 in Live to Ride, Ride to Live

Like all right thinking peoples, I like to ride the train. And I particularly like riding the train when there’s a brand new motorcycle waiting for me at the other end of the line.

Of course, a good book is a great way to pass the time if the scenery isn’t grabbing you.

It makes me very, very happy to have this in my driveway:

Niggles

As I have mentioned elsewhere, the turn signal switch has very little feel to it.

Also, the fuel neck is quite long, making it very difficult to tell when the tank is full.

Really hard to come up with negatives with this bike!
