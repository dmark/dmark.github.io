Horizon Zero Dawn

By: Mark Drummond On: Tue 28 March 2017
In: Vidya
Tags: #vidya

The Highs

  • Aloy's origin story is excellent. One of my all-time favourite video game storylines.

The Sighs

  • The fetch quests.
  • Combat: The spear feels useless outside stealth. The non-stealth combat loop, hitting machines with the right elemental types, trying to knock off critical components, etc., got old fast.

Worth It?

Absolutely.
