The First (real) Post

Date Sun 20 July 2014 Tags data / analyst

Welcome!

My name is Mark. I’m a career IT systems analyst edging my way toward a second career in data analysis. In recent years I’ve been looking for a new challenge on my career path. I’ve also been working on an undergraduate degree in Economics, gearing my courses towards econometrics, computing and mathematics.

Combining my IT systems background, my academic studies, some excellent data related MOOCs and an opportunity to work with the nascent data team where I work, I have an exciting opportunity to turn this into something real.

I hope to use this site to record much of my journey from IT Systems Analyst to Data Analyst, highlighting some of my work in an online portfolio. If you are interested in some background information, please visit The About Page.
