New Pen Day - The Narwhal Peter Pen

Tue 05 October 2021

Peters Draws is a super entertaining YouTuber whose videos are largely him drawring doodles with a fountain pen. Peter teamed up with Narwhal for this exclusive black-white-and-grey version of the classic Narwhal pen. Naturely, as a big fan of Peter Draws, I had to have one.

And holy smokes is this a great pen. It maybe writes a bit wide for my tastes but it is the smoothest nib I own by far. This is probably the physically largest pen I own. It holds a ton of ink in its full-body piston fill mechanism.

If only I could drawr like Peter!
