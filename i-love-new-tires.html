I Love New Tires (and Routine Maintenance)

Posted on August 27, 2017 in motorcycling

Closing in on 20,000kms on my 2016 R1200GS, and with my moto trip to Newfoundland looming, I booked the bike in for its 20,000km service. I really enjoy taking my vehicles in for routine maintenance. It’s surprisingly satisfying.

Even more satisfying is new tires. I ordered Metzeler Roadtec 01s to replace my stock Anakee 3s.

I had the work done at Endras BMW Motorrad in Ajax, Ontario. The service tech there was great. I mentioned the small amount of oil on my left fork. The clip that holds the seal in place (I really have no idea what I am talking about here) was not installed correctly, so the service tech fixed it up and told me to keep an eye out for any further leakage.
