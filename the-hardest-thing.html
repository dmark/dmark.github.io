The Hardest Thing

Date Sat 26 July 2014 Tags life / nihla

The hardest thing I have ever had to do in my life was saying goodbye to my 7 year old Sheltie, Nihla. Far too late we found out that she was in the very advanced stages of chronic kidney failure. She was far too young to suffer this fate, and certainly to young to be taken away from me. I was, and remain, in no way prepared to deal with this. It seems as though one moment she was fine, her normal self, and the next moment we were having to make an end-of-life decision. I continue to be shocked by her absence. Every day I imagine that I just need to open the front door and there you will be, waiting for me to let you in the house.

We all miss you Nihla.
