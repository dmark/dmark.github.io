New Squat PR

Date Mon 21 July 2014 Tags weightlifting / pr

We just completed a cycle of volume work at the gym. This week we are chasing new PRs. I haven’t attempted a new squat PR since March when I managed 290lbs. Even then, 290lbs is only a recent PR, not my all-time best.

On Monday I hit 315lbs which is both a recent and all time PR, so I am pretty happy about that. The lift felt good and I’m sure I could manage 325lbs or more. There is a faint glimmer at the end of a long tunnel that looks like a 405lb squat.

Looking forward to a new Clean PR. Like my squat, it has been a while, but I have a shot at finally making 100kg. As long as I have the, and please excuse the vernacular, testicular fortitude to get under the bar.
